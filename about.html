About

My story

Hi, my name is Gary and I am a User Experience Designer. I currently attend the University of Southern California as a business student in the MBV program at the Business School of Marshall with an expected graduation date of May 2022. I spent 5 years in the U.S. Air Force in various roles that helped me become an adaptive leader with great communication skills that is effective at solving problems. I believe in adding value to whatever organization, team, or program that I am a part of and I believe in my name being a brand that exemplifies integrity, diversity, innovation, and initiative. Since my time in the military has ended, I completed my Bachelor's degree at Arizona State University in Organizational Leadership. After recruiting UI, UX, and Product Designers for a living, I decided to become a designer myself and recently completed the UX Design course with Career Foundry. I am currently looking for a new opportunity to grow as a designer and am open to relocating. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my dog, working my way through new recipes I find online, and traveling to see new places.
